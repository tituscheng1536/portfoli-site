How it works

A closed loop between the market and the model.

Most systems are built once and left to decay. Portfoli's engine keeps re-evaluating strategy parameters against current market behavior, closing the loop every month instead of leaving it open indefinitely.

01

Deploy

Strategies run live on MT5 and NinjaTrader with full long and short execution.

02

Monitor

Performance and market regime data are logged continuously across all live strategies.

03

Retrain

The AI engine searches for robust, non-overfit parameter sets based on recent conditions.

04

Redeploy

Updated parameters are pushed live, and the loop begins again the following month.

The strategy suite

Trending and consolidating markets. One AI engine.

Trend-following and mean-reversion logic combined, so the portfolio isn't dependent on a single kind of market condition.

BDT Momentum · Trending

Identifies and rides directional price moves once they gain confirmed strength — entering with the trend and managing exits as momentum fades.

CMR Consolidation · Mean reversion

Identifies range-bound, low-volatility conditions and trades the reversion back toward equilibrium after price extends beyond its typical range.

VMR In progress

Reads volume behavior inside consolidating ranges to time entries around genuine accumulation and distribution, not just price. Currently in development.

MORPHEUS Trending

Captures sustained directional moves over longer horizons, using adaptive parameters to stay in a trend and reduce exposure once it breaks down.

About

Our mission

“Portfoli exists to build an AI engine that powers both trending and consolidating strategies — creating consistent profitability while trading with the discipline of an experienced manual trader.”

  • No martingale, ever. Position sizing never scales up to chase a loss.
  • No excessive trading. The engine takes trades because the math supports them, not to stay busy.
  • 1:1+ risk-to-reward, always. Every trade is structured so the potential win is worth at least as much as the risk.
  • No luck. All math. Every parameter is chosen because it generalizes — not because it got lucky in a backtest.

FAQ

What is Portfoli?

An AI-native quantitative trading engine running proprietary algorithmic strategies on MT5 and NinjaTrader, combining trend-following and mean-reversion logic with monthly, AI-assisted parameter retraining.

When will this product be available?

Portfoli is currently in its final testing phase, and is only open to private investors who'd like to try an early-stage product. We'll open access more broadly once testing wraps up.

Can I use this on a prop firm account?

Not without a special arrangement. Our strategies are already running live on select prop firms, and only so many accounts can follow the same trades before it risks getting everyone — including us — kicked off. Reach out if you'd like to discuss prop-firm access.

What platforms does Portfoli support?

MetaTrader 5 (MT5) and NinjaTrader.

How often are strategies updated?

Parameters are retrained on a monthly cycle, searching for robust parameter sets based on recent market behavior.

What markets does Portfoli trade?

Current profitability is concentrated in gold and silver, with active expansion into additional futures, commodities, and currency markets.
